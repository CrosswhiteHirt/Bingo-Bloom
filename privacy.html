Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lucky Bingo handles information when you use the game. If you have questions, contact us at 508923216@qq.com.

Information We Collect

Lucky Bingo stores gameplay progress, level completion, stars, score, and audio settings locally on your device. This local gameplay data is used only to provide game features and is not directly sent to us by the game.

Advertising and Third-Party SDKs

The app may use advertising and security SDKs. These services may process device information, advertising identifiers, app interaction data, diagnostics, approximate region, and ad performance data to provide, measure, and improve ads and app security. When required by the platform, the app asks for your permission before using the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA).

Advertising services integrated in the app may include platforms such as Chartboost, InMobi, Liftoff/Vungle, and Yandex Mobile Ads. Their data practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

Internet Access

Core gameplay is designed as a single-player experience. Internet access may be used to request ad configuration, load ads, support security services, and improve reliability.

Children's Privacy

Lucky Bingo is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

Data Retention

Local gameplay progress remains on your device until you delete the app or clear the app data. Third-party advertising and security partners may retain information according to their own policies.

Your Choices

You can control tracking permission in iOS Settings. You can also limit ad tracking through your device settings where available. Deleting the app removes locally stored game progress from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
